Microservices Quiz
1. What are Microservices?
Microservice architecture is an architectural style that structures an application as a collection of services that are Independently deployable, Loosely coupleds, Owned by a small team.

2. What are the benefits of using Microservices?
microservices architecture offers numerous benefits, including scalability, resilience, agility, flexibility, reusability, modularization, and improved collaboration.

3.What are the drawbacks of using microservices?
 complexity, testing and monitoring difficulties, communication overhead, and coordination challenges across small teams.
